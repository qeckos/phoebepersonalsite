    • Hello, World.

    I'm Phoebe Yang, a Global Business and Digital Arts student at the University of Waterloo! This program is everything that I love doing wrapped into one program! From hands-on experience with filmography and photgraphy, we learn how to work cameras and direct creative pieces such as documentaries. In addition to this, we get a ton of experience in the UX/UI field, through actual graphic design assignments and studying usability of modern interactions.

    WHO AM I?

    I'm a Chinese-Canadian person who has loved games, movies, music and art since I was a kid. The idea of creative worlds being built through just one song or game has drawn me deeply towards being a part of the teams that make music or game building possible. I love building these creative communities most of all.

    If I'm not in the studio designing, I'm out mingling and talking to new people! I love seeing other creative people and interacting with other people's work.
